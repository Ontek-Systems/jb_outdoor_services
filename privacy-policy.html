Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

At M.V. Garden Services, we keep things simple and respect your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle the information you provide when you get in touch with us.

1 Information We Collect

We only collect the information you voluntarily provide through our contact form. This includes:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Details regarding your garden project

2 How We Use It

We don't use any tracking cookies, Google Analytics, or third-party marketing tools.

The information you submit is sent directly to Matt, and is strictly used to review your enquiry, call or email you back, and get your gardening project booked in. We do not do anything else with it.

3 Data Sharing

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone. It stays securely with M.V. Garden Services for the sole purpose of carrying out the requested work.
