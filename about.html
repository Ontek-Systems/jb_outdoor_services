About Us

Rooted in Hertfordshire

The Founder

Expert garden care with a personal touch.

M.V. Garden Services was built to give Hertfordshire homeowners a gardening service they can genuinely rely on. We believe a well-kept garden is the heart of any home, which is why we approach every job with pride, care, and an eye for detail.

Founded by Matt Vinci, the business is proudly run as an independent operation by design. This means when you hire us, you’re getting the owner’s direct expertise, ensuring a consistently high standard of work on every single visit.

Why Choose Us

More than just cutting grass.

Finding a dependable gardener shouldn't be a hassle. Large companies often lose that personal touch, while casual workers can fall short on professional standards. M.V. Garden Services offers the perfect balance.

As a dedicated, independent business, we guarantee that the person you speak to on the phone is the exact same person who arrives at your gate. No subcontractors and no rushed visits; just honest, high-quality gardening you can rely on.

From full garden clearances to routine hedge trimming, our philosophy is simple: we treat your garden with the exact same care and attention as we would our own.
