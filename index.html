FAQs

Ask Us Anything
What areas do you cover?
We are based in Wincanton, Somerset, and serve all areas within a 10-mile radius. This includes Bruton, Castle Cary, Shepton Mallet, Templecombe, Gillingham, and surrounding villages. If you are unsure whether we cover your location, just give Jack a call and we will let you know.
Do you offer free quotes?
Yes, absolutely. All quotes from JB Outdoor Services Ltd are completely free and come with no obligation whatsoever. Jack will visit your site, assess the project in person, and provide a clear, detailed quote so you know exactly what to expect before any work begins.
Are you fully insured?
Yes. JB Outdoor Services Ltd is fully insured for all the landscaping, groundworks, and outdoor services we carry out. You can book with complete confidence knowing your property and our team are properly protected throughout every project.
What size diggers do you have available for hire?
We offer digger hire with a skilled operator for both a 1-tonne micro digger and a 2.6-tonne digger. The micro digger is ideal for tight access gardens and precise work, while the 2.6-tonne machine is suited to heavier excavation tasks such as foundation trenches, drainage, and ground levelling.
Do you supply firewood for delivery?
Yes. We supply both seasoned and unseasoned firewood logs and offer delivery for domestic customers as well as wholesale quantities for businesses. Contact Jack to discuss your requirements and arrange a delivery within our service area.
What are your working hours?
We operate Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. You are welcome to call or email outside of these hours and Jack will get back to you at the earliest opportunity.
Can you handle both domestic and commercial projects?
Yes. We work with both homeowners and commercial clients across Somerset. Whether it is a private garden makeover, a drainage project for a business premises, or a large-scale groundworks contract, we have the equipment and experience to deliver to a high standard.
How do I get started?
Simply call Jack on +44 7445 064666 or drop us an email at jboutdoorservicesltd@gmail.com. Alternatively, fill in the contact form on our website and we will get back to you promptly to discuss your project and arrange your free site visit.
