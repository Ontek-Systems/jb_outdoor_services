The Story

Who We Are

JB Outdoor Services Ltd was founded by Jack Banfield with a simple belief: outdoor work should be done properly, by people who take pride in it. Based in Wincanton, Somerset, Jack leads every project personally with a hands-on approach and a genuine commitment to quality and reliability.

From full garden transformations and hard landscaping to complex groundworks and drainage solutions, we bring the same level of skill and care to every job, whether it is a small residential patio or a large commercial site clearance.

We work with a skilled, reliable team and our own machinery, including 1-tonne micro and 2.6-tonne diggers with trained operators, so we can handle the full scope of any outdoor project from the ground up. No subcontractors, no shortcuts, just honest, high-quality work delivered on time.
