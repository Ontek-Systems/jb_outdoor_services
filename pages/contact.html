What areas around Wincanton do you cover?
We cover all towns and villages within a 10-mile radius of Wincanton, Somerset. This includes Bruton, Castle Cary, Shepton Mallet, Templecombe, Gillingham, Milborne Port, Sparkford, North and South Cadbury, and the surrounding rural areas. If you are unsure whether we reach you, just give Jack a call and he will confirm straight away.
How does the process work from first contact to finished project?
Simply get in touch by phone or email and Jack will arrange a free site visit to assess your project and discuss exactly what you want to achieve. You will receive a clear, honest quote with no hidden costs. Once you are happy to proceed, we will schedule the work to suit you and keep you informed throughout.
Do you offer free quotes?
Yes, all quotes from JB Outdoor Services Ltd are completely free and come with absolutely no obligation. Jack will visit your site, assess the scope of work in person, and provide a clear, detailed quote so you know exactly what is involved before any work begins.
What size diggers do you have and what can they be used for?
We operate a 1-tonne micro digger and a 2.6-tonne digger, both provided with a skilled operator. The micro digger is ideal for tight access gardens, precise planting areas, and confined spaces. The 2.6-tonne machine handles heavier work such as foundation trenches, drainage installation, ground levelling, and concrete pad preparation.
Can you handle both small domestic jobs and larger commercial projects?
Yes. We work with homeowners on everything from a single patio to a full garden transformation, and with businesses on larger groundworks, drainage, and site clearance contracts. Whatever the scale, we bring the same level of skill and care to every job.
Do you supply and deliver firewood?
Yes. We supply both seasoned and unseasoned logs for domestic and wholesale use, with delivery available throughout our service area. Contact Jack to discuss your requirements and arrange delivery.
Are you fully insured?
Yes, JB Outdoor Services Ltd is fully insured for all the landscaping, groundworks, and outdoor services we carry out. You can book with complete confidence that your property and our team are properly protected on every project.
What are your working hours?
We work Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM and on Saturdays from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. You are welcome to call or email outside these hours and Jack will get back to you at the earliest opportunity.
