Last updated: April 2025

At JB Outdoor Services Ltd, we keep things simple and respect your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle the information you provide when you get in touch with us.

We only collect the information you voluntarily provide through our contact form. This includes:

2 How We Use It

We don't use any tracking cookies, Google Analytics, or third-party marketing tools.

3 Data Sharing

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone. It stays securely with JB Outdoor Services Ltd for the sole purpose of carrying out the requested work.

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. Simply get in touch and we'll action your request promptly.
